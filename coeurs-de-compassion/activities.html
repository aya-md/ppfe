Food Assistance

The monthly basket

In our ongoing commitment to support widows in need, our charitable association provides a monthly basket of essential food supplies. With each delivery, we aim to alleviate the burden of hunger and ensure that these resilient women have access to nutritious meals.

Food Assistance

Ramadan's basket

During the holy month of Ramadan, our association extends a compassionate hand to widows in need, delivering baskets filled with essential food supplies. With each basket, we not only provide sustenance for iftars and suhoors but also offer a gesture of solidarity and support during this sacred time.

Education

Support and reinforcement lessons

our association stands in solidarity with orphans, offering reinforcement lessons to equip them with the skills and knowledge needed to excel. With dedicated tutors and personalized support, we create a nurturing environment where every orphan can thrive academically.

Medical & Health

Healthcare for widows and orphans

With a compassionate commitment to the well-being of widows and orphans, our association extends vital support through comprehensive medical check-ups. By ensuring access to healthcare services, we not only address immediate health needs but also empower individuals to lead healthier, more secure lives.

Entertainment

Orphan's Day

In honor of Orphan's Day, our charitable association orchestrates a heartfelt celebration to raise awareness about the challenges faced by orphans. Through joyful gatherings and meaningful activities, we shine a light on their resilience and courage amidst adversity.

Entertainment

Eid Ceremony

As part of our commitment to spreading joy and compassion, our charitable association organizes Eid ceremonies, dedicated to bringing smiles to the faces of orphans. Through this heartfelt activity, we aim to share the spirit of Eid by providing these children with new clothing, symbolizing a fresh start and a sense of belonging.

Education

Orphan's Backpack

At the start of each school year, our charitable association hosts the Orphans Backpack Activity, a special event to support kids in need. We gather backpacks filled with all the essentials for school, ensuring every child has what they need to thrive in the classroom. It's our way of showing these young learners that they're not alone, and that we're here to help them succeed.

Food Assistance

Eid Udhiyah

As Eid al-Adha draws near, our charitable association organizes the heartfelt tradition of Eid Udhiya, ensuring that widows within our community are not forgotten during this sacred time. With compassion as our guide, we distribute sheep to these resilient women, enabling them to partake in the joyous spirit of sacrifice and celebration.
