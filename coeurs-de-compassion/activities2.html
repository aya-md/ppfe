Food assistance

The monthly basket

It's a collection of essential food items (flour, oil,sugar,tea,etc.) distributed to needy orphan families.

Food assistance

Ramadan's basket

It's the same as the monthly basket but with more food items consumed during the holy month of Ramadan.

Education

Support and reinforcement lessons

the association aims through this process to identify the academic difficulties of orphan students and work on overcoming them with the help of experienced educational and competent teachers.

Medical & Health

Healthcare for widows and orphans

the medical services are provided for free for the benefit of widows and orphans through the coordination of volunteer doctors.

Entertainment

Orphan's Day

It is an activity celebrated by the association.The aim of this day is to raise awareness about the living conditions of widows and orphans.This event includes cultural and entertainment activities showcasing the creativity of the association's orphans.

Entertainment

Eid's Ceremony

It is an activity that aims to bring joy and happiness to the hearts of orphans and to share the joy of Eid with them by providing them with new clothings.

Education

Orphan's Backpack

It is an activity carried out by the association at the beginning of each school year, where a number of Backpacks with a set of school supplies are provided to those in need.

Food assistance

Eid Udhiyah

In an effort to share the joy of Eid al-Adha with orphaned and widowed families, the organization helps benefactors to purchase a group of sacrificial animals and distribute them to needy families of orphans and widows.
